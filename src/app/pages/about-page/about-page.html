MyHomeClinic App

Healthcare in
Your Pocket

MyHomeClinic App — the first medical application that digitalized the brain of a doctor, which has been the longstanding roadblock to digital health development. Through MyHomeClinic Mobile and its web component, indisputable medical diagnosis can be projected wherever there is no doctor.

What makes MyHomeClinic different?

Unlike conventional health apps that merely log data, MyHomeClinic replicates clinical decision-making logic — enabling real diagnostic conclusions in remote and underserved communities where physician access is absent.
